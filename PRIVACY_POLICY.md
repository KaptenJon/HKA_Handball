# Privacy Policy – HKA Handball

**Last updated:** 2025-01-01

## Overview

HKA Handball is a free, offline handball game. We respect your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any data.** Specifically:

- ❌ No personal information collected
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No advertising
- ❌ No internet connection required or used
- ❌ No cookies or local storage of personal data
- ❌ No third-party services

## Permissions

The app requires **no special permissions**. It runs entirely offline on your device.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

If this policy changes, it will be updated in this repository.
